Zero Data Collection

Pesums does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following information:

We never see what websites you visit, how long you stay, or what you do there. Your browsing activity is completely invisible to us.

Your Search Queries

Your Device Information

Your Personal Information

No email addresses, no phone numbers, no names, no demographic data. We don't ask for it, we don't want it.

Protection from Online Threats

Your safety online goes beyond privacy. Pesums actively protects you from a wide range of digital threats that could compromise your security, steal your data, or harm your device. Our multi-layered protection system works silently in the background to keep you safe.

Pesums Shield: Your Digital Bodyguard

Pesums Shield is our comprehensive protection layer that blocks threats before they reach you. Every website you visit is automatically scanned and filtered through our security systems.

Ad Blocker

Blocks all advertisements including banner ads, pop-ups, video ads, and native ads. Not only does this give you a cleaner browsing experience, but it also prevents malvertising—malicious ads that can infect your device or steal your data.

Tracker Blocker

Stops tracking scripts from companies like Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and thousands of other trackers that follow you across the web. These trackers build detailed profiles of your behavior—Pesums makes sure they can't see you.

Cookie Protection

Automatically blocks third-party cookies that websites use to track you across different sites. First-party cookies (needed for basic website functionality) are allowed, but cross-site tracking cookies are eliminated.

Anti-Fingerprinting

Prevents websites from creating a unique "fingerprint" of your browser based on your fonts, screen resolution, plugins, and other characteristics. This advanced tracking technique is completely blocked by Pesums.

Malware Protection

Scans links and downloads for known malware, viruses, ransomware, and other malicious software. Dangerous sites are blocked before they can harm your device or steal your information.

Phishing Defense

Identifies and blocks fake websites designed to steal your passwords, credit card numbers, or personal information. Our constantly updated database of phishing sites protects you from sophisticated scams.

HTTPS Enforcement

Automatically upgrades all connections to secure HTTPS whenever possible. This encrypts your data in transit, preventing eavesdropping, man-in-the-middle attacks, and data interception.

Script Injection Protection

Blocks malicious scripts that websites might try to inject into your browser to exploit vulnerabilities, steal data, or compromise your system.

Cryptojacking Prevention

Stops websites from secretly using your computer's processing power to mine cryptocurrency without your permission, protecting your device's performance and electricity.

Real-Time Threat Intelligence

Pesums maintains and constantly updates our threat database using anonymous, aggregated security intelligence. When a new threat is identified anywhere in the world, our entire user base is protected within minutes—without compromising anyone's privacy.

No Tracking. No Profiling. No Following.

Most browsers claim to offer "privacy mode" while still tracking you in countless ways. Pesums is different. We block every known tracking method:

  • No cookies tracking: Third-party cookies that follow you across websites are completely blocked.
  • No pixel tracking: Invisible tracking pixels that report your activity are prevented from loading.
  • No browser fingerprinting: Websites cannot create unique identifiers based on your browser configuration.
  • No canvas fingerprinting: Advanced fingerprinting techniques using HTML5 canvas are blocked.
  • No WebGL fingerprinting: Graphics card fingerprinting is prevented.
  • No audio fingerprinting: Your audio stack cannot be used to identify you.
  • No font fingerprinting: Your installed fonts remain private.
  • No cross-site tracking: You cannot be followed from one website to another.
  • No behavioral profiling: Your browsing patterns stay private.

How Pesums Works Without Your Data

You might wonder: if we don't collect data, how does Pesums function? The answer is simple—everything happens locally on your device. Your bookmarks, history (if you choose to keep one), passwords, and settings are all stored on your computer, encrypted and private. Pesums never sends this information to our servers because we don't have servers collecting user data.

Technical Guarantee: Pesums is architected so that user data never leaves your device. This isn't a policy we could change—it's how the software is built.

Open Source & Transparent

Pesums is built on open-source technology, which means our code is publicly available for anyone to inspect. Security researchers, privacy advocates, and developers worldwide can verify that we do exactly what we say we do. No backdoors. No hidden trackers. No secret data collection. When we say we protect your privacy, you don't have to take our word for it—you can verify it yourself.

You're in Complete Control

While Pesums doesn't collect your data, we give you full control over the data that stays on your device:

  • Clear browsing data anytime: Delete your local history, cache, and cookies whenever you want.
  • Export your data: Take your bookmarks and settings with you if you switch devices or browsers.
  • Private browsing mode: Browse without saving any local history at all.
  • Sync settings (optional): If you choose to sync settings across devices, data is end-to-end encrypted and we cannot read it.

No Third Parties. No Exceptions.

We don't share data with third parties because we don't have data to share. No analytics companies. No advertising networks. No data brokers. No government agencies (unless legally required with a valid warrant for specific criminal investigations, and even then, we have nothing to provide). No exceptions.

Children's Privacy

Pesums is safe for users of all ages. Because we don't collect any personal information, we don't have special requirements for children's data—we treat everyone's privacy equally: we protect it absolutely.
