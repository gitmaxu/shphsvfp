Core Protection

Pesums Shield —
Your Digital Armor

The flagship security feature engineered to deliver uncompromising privacy during every browsing session. Everything processed locally. Zero internet communication.

Aggressive Blocking Mode
Proactively blocks trackers, bad scripts, malicious activity, and all intrusive ads before they load. Ideal for high-risk environments like public Wi-Fi. Enabled by default — no setup needed.
Standard Balanced Mode
Blocks the majority of threats while allowing non-intrusive elements for basic site functionality. Perfect for everyday browsing where full aggression might occasionally hinder legitimate sites.
Fingerprinting Protection
Actively obfuscates screen resolution, installed fonts, hardware configs — making it extremely difficult to build an accurate device fingerprint. Full randomization option available.
Cookie Control (All or 3rd-Party)
Full blocking eliminates 100% of all cookies. Third-party mode allows first-party session cookies while blocking cross-site tracking entirely. Both applied instantly, locally, no syncing.
Full Customization
Mix and match modes, create whitelists for trusted sites, review blocked items in real time. All configurations stored on-device. No cloud. No risk.
100% Local Processing
Unlike other browsers that send data to servers for threat detection, Pesums Shield processes everything locally. No data leaves your device. Ever. That's the foundation.

Is Pesums Browser secure? ∨
Absolutely. Pesums is built for maximum security. We block every tracker, cookie, and fingerprinting attempt by default. Your connection upgrades to HTTPS automatically. No third-party code. No data leaks. No surveillance. Just pure, protected browsing that puts your safety first.

Pesums is not available yet, but it's on the way. Soon it will work on Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS. Just click download, install in seconds, and start browsing privately. No account needed. No registration. No hassle. Freedom in one click.
Does Pesums block ads on YouTube? ∨
Yes. Pesums Shield blocks ads and trackers not only on YouTube, but across the entire internet. No website is whitelisted. Every domain is checked for tracking, analytics, ads, and harmful activity. Faster loading, zero distractions, and pure private browsing everywhere.

Pesums stands for Project Every Source URL Multiple Secure Services — a mission focused on protecting users through privacy, security, and trusted web defense.
What is Pesums Shield? ∨
Pesums Shield is our powerful protection layer. It automatically blocks ads, trackers, malware, and fingerprinting scripts before they load. Works silently. Works instantly. No configuration needed. Just complete protection on every site you visit.

Yes. Pesums is built on open-source technology with full transparency. Anyone can inspect our code. No hidden backdoors. No secret trackers. What you see is what you get — total privacy, verified by the community.

Pesums is lightning fast. By blocking ads and trackers by default, pages load up to 6x faster than Chrome. Less junk to download means quicker loading, longer battery life, and smoother browsing. Speed meets privacy.

No. Pesums never collects, stores, or shares your personal data. No tracking, no telemetry, no background monitoring. Only the websites you open communicate with the internet.
